Poco Phones

Poco X2

The POCO X2 features a 6.67-inch 120Hz display, Qualcomm Snapdragon 730G processor, and a quad-camera setup with a 64MP primary sensor. It also includes a dual front-facing camera, a 4500mAh battery with 27W fast charging, and options for 6GB or 8GB of RAM with 64GB, 128GB, or 256GB of storage. The phone runs on Android with MIUI 11 for POCO and has a side-mounted fingerprint sensor.

Poco X3

The POCO X3 is a mid-range smartphone featuring a 6.67-inch 120Hz display with Full HD+ resolution and is powered by a Snapdragon 732G processor. It has a 64MP quad-camera setup on the rear and a 20MP front-facing camera. It packs a large 6000mAh battery with 33W fast charging and comes with up to 8GB of RAM and 128GB of storage. The phone also includes a side-mounted fingerprint sensor, dual SIM support, and runs on MIUI 12 based on Android 10.

Poco X4

The Poco X4 Pro 5G features a 6.67-inch AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, powered by a Snapdragon 695 5G processor, and equipped with a 108MP main rear camera alongside an 8MP ultrawide and 2MP macro lens. It also includes a 16MP front-facing camera, a 5000mAh battery with 67W fast charging, and runs on Android 11 with MIUI 13. The device supports 5G, Wi-Fi 5, Bluetooth 5.1, and NFC.
